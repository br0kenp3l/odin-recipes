lasagna recipe

Description

Using healthy and very simple ingredients we will create a traditional, tasty lasagna. This is my grandmothers recipe and i know she would love me sharing it with the world!

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 pound beef mince
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 3 ounces of tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • oregano,garlic powder,sugar
  • white wine vinegar
  • lasagna sheets
  • ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Brown minced beef
  3. Cook bell pepper, onion and garlic then add back the beef
  4. Transfer to larger pan and add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingrediants, then add herbs and seasoning to taste
  5. Layer the pasta and sauce to create the lasagna
  6. layer all the cheese ontop
  7. bake at 375 farenheit for 45 min
