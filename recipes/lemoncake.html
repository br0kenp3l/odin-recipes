Lemon Cake Recipe

Description

A delicious, moist all in one lemon sponge cake. Perfect for a teatime treat, cake sale or as a birthday cake!

Ingredients

  • 225g of self raising flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 225g of soft butter
  • 225g caster sugar
  • zest of 2 lemons
  • 2 tsp milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees and line baking tins
  2. sift the flour and baking powder into a large bowl
  3. add remaining ingredients
  4. bake for 30-35 minutes
